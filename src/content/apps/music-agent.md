All my music tracks are available on **Spotify, Itunes, Apple Music &** **Soundcloud**.  
Produced by mITy.John using [MusicAgent](https://github.com/janvanwassenhove/MusicAgent), final edit and recording made by mITy.John in Sonic Pi.

_[MusicAgent](https://github.com/janvanwassenhove/MusicAgent) is a MAS (Multi-Agent-System) I developed for composing music while coding tracks in Sonic PI. It generates a complete new song, with lyrics and album cover based on user input using GPT agents._
