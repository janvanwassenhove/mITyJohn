mITyLex is my personal take on language learning — a focused, playful, and developer-built alternative to mainstream apps.

Created in the same creative playground behind the projects on **mityjohn.com**, mITyLex blends structured progression, smart gamification, and clean design into one simple idea: make consistent learning feel natural and motivating.

Short lessons. Clear goals. Visible progress.  
No noise — just forward momentum.

mITyLex is more than a language app.  
It’s an experiment in building meaningful digital products with code, curiosity, and a slightly obsessive drive to improve things that already “work.”
