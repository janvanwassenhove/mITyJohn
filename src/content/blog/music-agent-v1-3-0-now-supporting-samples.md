We’re thrilled to announce the release of **Music Agent v1.3.0**, a major update that takes your music composition to the next level with **sample support**!

## What’s New in v1.3.0?

**1\. Sample Selection and Integration**  
Samples are now seamlessly integrated into the creation chain, offering endless possibilities for creativity. Whether you’re layering beats, adding texture, or experimenting with unique sounds, this new feature opens up a world of opportunities.

**2\. Metadata Listing Script**  
Selecting the right sample is now easier than ever. With the new metadata listing script, you can generate a detailed overview of available samples based on your samples located in the ‘Samples’ directory. This way or agents can make an selections on sample to use during the song creation.

## Why Samples Matter

Samples allow you to enrich your compositions, create unique soundscapes, and experiment with new styles. Whether you’re a seasoned composer or just exploring, this feature ensures that your music stands out.
